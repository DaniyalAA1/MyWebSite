Resources for Computer Science

Professional, approved, and well written textbooks are the best way to learn a topic. You can go in depth on a subject, and learn at your own pace. Using online tutorials from random websites can compromise your learning. They might not be fact checked, trustworthy, or safe to use. Textbooks are a better option. However, videos online can be informative and beneficials to learn from.
